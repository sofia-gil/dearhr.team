Job Search Support

Feeling lost in your job search? Sending out dozens of applications and hearing nothing back?

This service is for professionals who are actively applying — but not getting interviews — or feel unsure about how to structure an effective search across markets or industries. We’ll audit your current strategy, uncover blind spots, and build a realistic roadmap based on your goals, market conditions, and personal strengths. No fluff — just practical insights that help you move forward.

What's included

  • 🔍 Strategy Session (written or live): We review your current job search, application tactics, and positioning.
  • 🎯 Target Market Alignment: Guidance on industries, companies, and roles aligned with your profile and strengths.
  • 🧩 Action Plan: Weekly roadmap with a personalized mix of platforms, tactics, and follow-ups — so you know exactly where to focus.
  • 📊 KPI Tracking Framework: Learn how to measure what’s working, what’s not, and how to iterate effectively.

How it Works:

  1. Submit Your Info: Fill out a quick form detailing your goals, experience, and current efforts.
  2. Receive Your Strategy: Get your personalized roadmap within 5 business days — or book a live session if preferred.
  3. Refine: One round of questions or clarification is included.

Who Is This For?

  • Job seekers who feel stuck, overwhelmed, or unsure where to focus their energy.
  • People applying internationally or across industries without getting results.
  • Professionals looking to be more intentional and strategic about their next move.

Packages and Pricing:

  • Written Strategy & Roadmap: 60 USD
  • Live Session + Strategy Plan: 90 USD

💡 Packages are personalized depending on your goals and needs — reach out if you'd like a custom bundle.

🌍 Languages to choose from: Spanish, English, Italian, Portuguese, Catalan, or Basque.

💳 Payments accepted in: EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, or NZD — converted at the USD equivalent at the time of payment.
