Networking Strategy & LinkedIn Outreach Scripts

Tired of sending connection requests and cold messages that go unanswered?

This service helps you build a strategic, sustainable networking approach — one that doesn’t feel fake, awkward, or random. You’ll learn how to reach out to recruiters, hiring managers, and industry peers in a way that actually gets responses. We’ll equip you with message templates tailored to your goals and audience, plus a framework for building long-term visibility and relationships on LinkedIn and beyond.

What's included

  • 💬 Outreach Message Scripts: Templates you can personalize to reach recruiters, hiring managers, or company contacts — adapted to different situations (inbound, referral, follow-up).
  • 🧭 Networking Strategy Brief: Who to contact, when to reach out, and how to build credibility without spamming.
  • 📈 LinkedIn Engagement Tips: Practical ideas to boost your visibility and stay top-of-mind without spending hours online.
  • 🛠️ Optional Audit: Feedback on your current profile or recent messages (if applicable).

How it Works:

  1. Submit Your Info: Share your job search goals and target audience via a short form or voice note.
  2. Receive Your Toolkit: Get your custom scripts and networking strategy within 4–5 business days.
  3. Refine: One round of edits included to make sure the scripts reflect your tone and context.

Who Is This For?

  • Professionals who want to stop relying only on job boards and start opening doors through networking.
  • Job seekers unsure how to message people they don’t know — or who are tired of being ignored.
  • People trying to break into new markets, industries, or roles and need to build relevant connections.

  • Full Networking Strategy + Scripts + Audit: 70 USD
