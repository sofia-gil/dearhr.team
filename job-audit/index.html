Job Application Audit

Sending out applications but not hearing back? You're not alone.

This service is for job seekers who’ve been actively applying but haven’t secured interviews — and are ready to find out why. We’ll audit your CV, cover letter, and recent applications to identify what’s blocking your results and how to fix it. You’ll get clear, personalized feedback based on recruiter standards and global hiring practices — so you can stop guessing and start improving.

What's included

  • 🔍 Application Materials Review: We analyze your CV, cover letter, and up to 3 job ads you’ve recently applied for.
  • 📊 Detailed Audit Report: You’ll receive a written report with specific, honest feedback on clarity, targeting, format, tone, and recruiter-readiness.
  • 🚧 Action Plan: We outline exactly what you need to adjust to increase your chances — from content to keywords and structure.
  • 📬 Email Q&A: One round of follow-up questions to clarify the feedback or get extra tips.

How it Works:

  1. Submit Your Materials: Send us your CV, cover letter, and links to up to 3 jobs you applied for.
  2. Receive Your Audit: Within 3–5 business days, we’ll send back your audit report and action plan.
  3. Ask Questions: You’ll have one round of follow-up via email to clarify any feedback or get additional guidance.

Who Is This For?

  • Job seekers applying consistently but not getting interviews.
  • People unsure if their materials are aligned with recruiter expectations.
  • Professionals who want expert eyes on their application before making major changes.

Packages and Pricing:

  • Standard Application Audit (1 CV + 2 job ads): 35 USD
  • Extended Audit (1 CV + 1 Cover Letter + LinkedIn + 3 job ads): 60 USD

💡 Packages are personalized depending on your goals and needs — reach out if you'd like a custom bundle.

🌍 Languages to choose from: Spanish, English, Italian, Portuguese, Catalan, or Basque.

💳 Payments accepted in: EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, or NZD — converted at the USD equivalent at the time of payment.
