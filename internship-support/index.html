Internship Support

Struggling to land your first internship — or not sure where to begin?

Whether you're a student, recent graduate, or career changer, this service is designed to help you stand out and confidently apply to internship opportunities in your target industry or country. We’ll work together to define your value, craft an internship-ready CV and cover letter, and create a customized application strategy tailored to your goals and strengths — even if you have limited experience.

What's included

  • 🎯 Internship Goal Mapping: We define your focus, industry preferences, and ideal location(s).
  • 📄 Internship CV & Cover Letter: A clear, compelling application package that highlights your motivation and potential.
  • 📌 Application Strategy: We help you identify opportunities, track applications, and optimize where and how you apply.
  • 📬 LinkedIn & Email Outreach Templates: Professionally written messages for cold outreach, networking, and follow-ups.

How it Works:

  1. Start with a Brief: Fill out a short form or send a voice note with your interests, background, and goals.
  2. Strategy + Documents: Within 5–7 business days, receive your tailored CV, cover letter, and internship application roadmap.
  3. Apply with Confidence: Use your new toolkit to apply independently — or add extra support if you want us to guide you through the process.

Who Is This For?

  • University students and recent grads looking for their first internship experience.
  • Career changers or adult learners who want structured support navigating internship applications.
  • Anyone who feels unsure how to present themselves without much formal experience.

Packages and Pricing:

  • CV + Cover Letter for Internships: 60 USD (1 language) / 85 USD (2 languages)
  • Full Internship Kit (CV, Cover Letter, LinkedIn + Outreach Templates): 110 USD (1 language) / 150 USD (2 languages)

💡 We specialize in European and international internship systems — from Erasmus+ to remote placements in global companies.

🌍 Languages to choose from: Spanish, English, Italian, Portuguese, Catalan, or Basque.

💳 Payments accepted in: EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, or NZD — converted at the USD equivalent at the time of payment.
