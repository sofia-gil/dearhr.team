CV Review & Redesign

Already have a CV, but not landing interviews?

This service is for professionals who feel like their CV might be holding them back — whether it’s outdated, generic, unfocused, or simply not aligned with the roles they’re targeting.

We’ll give your existing CV a complete overhaul — sharpening your positioning, updating your structure, and making sure your strengths come through clearly to both recruiters and ATS.

What's included

  • 🔍 Detailed CV Audit: We assess your existing document, spotting weaknesses in clarity, content, and formatting.
  • ✍️ Complete Rewrite: We revamp your CV from top to bottom — not just edits, but a full transformation.
  • 📌 Keyword & Role Targeting: Tailored wording and layout to match your goals and boost recruiter/ATS visibility.
  • 🎨 Professional Redesign: Clean, modern formatting in an editable layout (Word or Google Docs), aligned with today’s hiring trends.

How it Works:

  1. Send Your Current CV: Along with a short form or voice note to understand your goals and where you’re stuck.
  2. Deep-Dive Review: We audit the structure, language, and impact of your current version.
  3. CV Transformation: You receive a fully rewritten and redesigned version within 5–7 business days, plus one round of feedback edits.

Who Is This For?

  • Professionals who’ve applied to several jobs with no responses or interviews.
  • Job seekers unsure if their CV is aligned with their current goals, market, or industry.
  • Those using a generic CV that no longer reflects their growth, skills, or personality.

Packages and Pricing (USD):

  • 📝 Basic CV Redesign + Written Career Guidance: 40 USD (1 language) / 60 USD (2 languages)
  • 📞 Advanced CV Rewrite + Phone Career Guidance: 55 USD (1 language) / 80 USD (2 languages)
  • 🎯 Full Package (Advanced CV + Career Guidance + 2 Custom Cover Letters): 90 USD (1 language) / 140 USD (2 languages)

💡 Not sure if your CV needs a full rewrite or just light editing? Send it over — we’ll take a look and recommend the best path.

🌍 Languages to choose from: Spanish, English, Italian, Portuguese, Catalan, or Basque.

💳 Payments accepted in: EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, or NZD — converted at the USD equivalent at the time of payment.
